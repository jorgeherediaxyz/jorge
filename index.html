Jorge Heredia (Lima, 1958) is a Peruvian visual artist, sometimes designer, filmmaker, writer, translator, and/or genealogist; living in Amsterdam since 1984; unmarried to journalist and translator Heleen Sittig, with two children, Rafael and Paz.

He is also a regular swimmer, urban biker, cactus keeper, and an eager chef dans sa cuisine.

Jorge Heredia went to Colegio de la Inmaculada, Jesuits, Lima (1967-1975); studied at the Escuela de Artes Plásticas de la Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú, Lima (1976-1980); and majored in Film Production at Seton Hall University, South Orange, NJ, USA (1981-1984); he also followed a Webdesign training at Stichting Kreft, Amsterdam (2000-2002).

He has been active as photographer for many years. This is a small sample of his oeuvre.
